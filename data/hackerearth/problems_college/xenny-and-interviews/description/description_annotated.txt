Xenny had interviews scheduled for †D‡ consecutive days.
On each of the days, Xenny was rushing to his room for an interview, since the interview was going to begin in very less time. On reaching the room, he knocked on the door, but nobody opened it. His roommate, PowerShell was fast asleep.

Xenny suddenly reminded of what one of his friends had told him about PowerShell - he had said that if he knocks on the door †N‡ times, and if more than †T‡ knocks are having a strictly increasing order intensity, then PowerShell wakes up.
